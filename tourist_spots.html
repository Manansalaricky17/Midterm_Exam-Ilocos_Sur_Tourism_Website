Popular Tourist Spots

Calle Crisologo

A famous street in Vigan, Ilocos Sur, Philippines. It features Spanish Colonial architecture and is a major tourist attraction.

Location: Crisologo, Vigan City, Ilocos Sur, Philippines

Category: Historical, Cultural

Rating: 4.6/5 (based on 1,658 reviews)

Bantay Bell Tower

Bantay Bell Tower used to be the belfry of St. Augustine Parish Church (one of the oldest in Ilocos region established in 1590) and served as watchtower at the same time - against invading pirates - during the Spanish era, hence the term bantay.

Location: 003 Rizal Street, Bantay, Ilocos Sur,Philippines

Category: Historical, Natural, Cultural

Rating: 4.5/5 (based on 1,551 reviews)

Baluarte Resort and Mini Zoo

Baluarte Zoo is a zoological park in Vigan, Ilocos Sur, Philippines. It was established by politician and businessman, Chavit Singson.

Location: Quirino Boulevard, Tamag, Vigan City, Ilocos Sur, Philippines

Category: Natural

Rating: 4.1/5 (based on 5,416 reviews)
